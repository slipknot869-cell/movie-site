Director: Satoshi Kon

Genre: Psychological Thriller

A story about Mima, a pop idol who becomes an actress and struggles to separate reality from illusion as her fame attracts dangerous obsession.

Director: Katsuhiro Otomo

Genre: Sci-Fi / Cyberpunk

A dystopian story about biker gangs and psychic powers in post-apocalyptic Neo-Tokyo.

Director: Hayao Miyazaki

Genre: Fantasy / Adventure
